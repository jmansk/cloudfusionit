Disrupting IT, One Fix at a Time

This isn't a company (yet). It's a project. A lab. A rebellion against bloated, overcomplicated IT — and you're welcome to watch it unfold.

Why I Started This

I’ve worked in IT long enough to see how broken it is — especially for small businesses. Expensive tools, poor documentation, security bolt-ons that barely work. It doesn’t have to be this way.

CloudFusionIT started as a side project to simplify modern IT — not just for me, but for anyone else tired of the same mess.

What You’ll Find Here

Firewall & Security Builds

Hands-on guides using OPNsense, Entra, VPNs, segmentation and real-world configs built for CE/CE+ compliance.

Automation & Scripting

PowerShell, Bash and CLI-based workflows for real-world MSP and admin use. Time-saving tools, not theory.

Cloud-First Strategy

Smart design for small businesses using Microsoft 365, Intune, and Azure — without the enterprise complexity.

Compliance Made Practical

Cyber Essentials and CE+ guidance written for people who actually have to implement it, not just check a box.

Where This Is Headed

This isn't just content. It’s groundwork. The long-term goal is to build tools, guides, and systems that actually serve SMBs — lean, secure, automated. No fluff. No noise. Just smart solutions that work.

If you’re here early, you’re part of the build process. Watch what’s coming. Or better — borrow what works and make it your own.
