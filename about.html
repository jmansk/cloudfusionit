About Me

Hi, I’m James – an IT support specialist and automation enthusiast based in the UK. I’ve spent the last 10 years helping businesses of all sizes streamline their systems, secure their infrastructure, and make technology work on their terms.

Over the years, I’ve worked with major organisations like Bauer Media and LCP, gaining deep experience with enterprise infrastructure. But my passion lies in helping small businesses. Too often they’re stuck with overpriced, overcomplicated tech – and I know it doesn’t have to be that way.

CloudFusionIT is about bringing that enterprise clarity and stability to smaller teams. It’s a project born out of frustration and curiosity – a space to build, experiment, and share better ways to manage modern IT.

I hold a BTEC National Diploma in IT (Software Development) and have always been drawn to logic, automation, and systems thinking. Whether it's scripting, Intune, cloud security, or infrastructure – I'm always refining, automating, and simplifying.

If you’re working on something and think we might be aligned – or if you just want to swap ideas – get in touch. I’d love to hear from you.
